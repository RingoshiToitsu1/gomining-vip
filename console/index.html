Frequently asked questions

Is GoMining profitable?

GoMining profitability depends on the Bitcoin price, network difficulty and your miner's efficiency (watts per TH). GMT Optimizer projects all three with live data so you can see your break-even and ROI before you invest — including the downside.

What is GMT and how does the discount work?

GMT is GoMining's token. Locking GMT (or holding it in your wallet) builds coverage that discounts your daily electricity fee in 1% steps, up to a 20% cap, and unlocks VIP tiers. The planner finds the optimal amount to lock.

How is GoMining ROI calculated?

The tool computes daily mining rewards (sats per TH per day) from live network data, subtracts your electricity fee after any GMT discount, and projects the result forward through halvings and difficulty growth to estimate payback time.
